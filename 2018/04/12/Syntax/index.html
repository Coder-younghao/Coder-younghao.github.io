Adverbial

  • 修饰性状语(adjuncts)
    • 时间状语：He was born on the morning of May 4th, 1990.
    • 地点状语：She sat at a table in a restaurant
    • 过程状语：He told me in person at the meeting yesterday.
    • 原因状语：Being unable to concentrate at home, he went to the library.
    • 目的状语：He ran away lest he should be seen
    • 让步状语：Rich as he was, he died a most unhappy man.
    • 条件状语： You will certainly succeed if you work harder.
  • 评注性状语 (disjuncts)
    • 评注性状语包括文体评注性状语和内容评注性状语。前者有:briefly, broadly, candidly, frankly, honestly 等。后者有:actually, certainly, hopefully, in fact, maybe, perhaps, possibly, undoubtedly, actually, theoretically 等。
  • 连接性状语 (conjuncts)

Relative Clauses

  • 限定性
    • (根据关系性引导词分成几类，常见的关联词有关系代词 that, which, who, whom, whose 和关系副词 where, when, why 等，切记 what 不能引导定语从句。)
    • The man that/who is speaking at the meeting is an advanced worker.
  • 非限定性
    • 非限制性定语从句起补充说明作用，缺少也不会影响全句的理解。非限制性定语从句不能用 that 或 why 作为引导词。在非限制性定语从句的前面往往有逗号隔开。
    • This novel, which I have read three times, is very touching.

一致(Concord)

  • 动词一致通常为三个互为补充的原则所支配，即语法一致原则、意义一致原则和就近一致原则。

    • Everybody was happy with the decision. (语法一致)
    • The audience were brought to their feet at the sight of superman in his wheelchair.(意义一致)
    • Either you or I am responsible for the mistake. (就近一致)

  • 在数学公式中，仅两数相加，动词谓语可以用单数，也可以用 复数;两数相减、相乘和相除，动词谓语通常用单数。

  • 关系分句的先行项为 “one of + 复数名词”时,根据就近一致的原则,动词与靠近它的复数名词(而不是 one)一致
  • 当 one 的前面被 the only 修饰时,关系分句的先行项是 one 而不是复数名词,依据语法一致的原则,动词应用单数形式

Coordination and Subordination

  • Coordination
    • and，or，but
    • English is difficult to learn，and so is French
    • Liz is talkative but her husband is reserved
    • Clean up the mess，or else!
  • Subordination
    • Wh-词:how(ever), what(ever), when(ever), where(ever), whether, which, while, who(ever), whom, whose, why 等
      • 其他:after, although, as, because, before, for, if, lest, once, since, that, though, till, unless, until 等
      • The man who is smoking a cigar is a film director.
      • When I was young, I listened to the radio waiting for my favourite song.
      • What he does is often at variance with what he says.

Information Structure

  • My dog sent the mail. — The mail was sent by my dog.(被动态passive)
  • To make fun of a disabled man is not funny at a11.— It is not funny at all to make funny of a disabled man.(外位Extraposition)
  • The time when anyone can afford a car will come.
    — The time will come when anyone can afford a car.(分隔discontinuity)
  • Into a large crowd of spectators the plane dived. (前移Fronting)
  • Never have I seen such a rude man（倒装inversion）
  • She enjoying going swimming and so does her daughter.（主语也..）
  • It is John that/who/whom you can consult.（分裂cleaving）
