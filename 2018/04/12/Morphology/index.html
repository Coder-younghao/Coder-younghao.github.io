Noun、Pronoun、Number

Noun

  • 按含义分
    • 专有：Africa， Monica， MacDonald
    • 普通：Apple， book，aeroplane
  • 按是否可数分
    • 可数：An apple, a cup, three floors
    • 不可数：Milk, coffee, bread

Pronoun

  • Personal pron：I，you，we，they，it
  • Demonstrative pron：this，these，that
  • Relative pron：which，who，that
  • Interrogative pron：who，which，what
  • Indefinite pron：some，none，few，many
  • Reflexible pron：myself，yourself
  • Possessive pron：mine，yours，his
  • Reciprocal pron：each，other，one another
  • Intensive pron：myself，yourselves

Number

  • singular invariable
    • proper noun
      • 绝大多数是单数名词。但也有一些以复数形式出现，如
      • 指家庭时：The Blacks are away on holidays.
      • 多个人有同一姓名：There are three Mikes in our class
      • 某些国家名：The philippines is/are an island country
    • common noun
      • 绝大多数是单数名词
      • -ics 词尾表学科为单数：classics，mathematics，physics
      • -s 词尾表疾病为单数：arthritis，diabetes，shingles
      • -s 词尾表游戏为单数：billiards，bowls，darts

  • plural invariable

    • 一些有对称两部分的服装或工具：Glasses，jeans，pants
    • 集合名词：Cattle，clergy，people
    • 山脉，瀑布群，群岛名：The Alps，the Himalayas
    • 以-s 结尾的名词：Clothes，goods，minutes

  • Irregular plurals

Determiner、Genitive

Determiner

  • Indefinite Article（a，an）
    • There is a policeman at the door.
    • A car must be insured.
  • Definite Article（the）
    • The horse is a useful animal.
    • the Chinese，the rich

Genitive

  • ‘s，用于指人的名词
  • of短语，用于指物和无生命的物体

Adjective phrase

  • 前置修饰语顺序：县官行令谢国材
  • 比较结构
    • as..as../so..as..： He is as/so tall as his brother
    • 比较级+than

Prepositions

  • 和地点有关的

    • at：Peter is at school.
    • in：She is lying in bed.
    • on：She is walking dangerously on the rope
    • above&below：He waited below the window until she opened it and spoke to him.
    • over&under：She leaned over the river to see her reflection in the water
    • between：She sat between the two children.
    • among：She sat among the three children
    • aboard：通常和指代一种交通工具或特定车辆的名字连用
    • from：后面可以跟另外一个介词或副词，表示运动的出发点

  • 和时间有关的介词

    • at：at five o’clock/midnight/noon/his uncle’s death
    • on：on Sunday/weekdays/May 1st
    • in：in the morning/afternoon/evening
  • 表示其他关系的介词
    • aim a gun at sb. (目标)
    • pray for peace (目的)
    • buy sth. for sb. (指定目的)
    • keep healthy by jogging (方式)
    • This photo was taken by a child. (施事)
    • cover one’s body with a blanket (工具)
    • spend time with sb. (陪伴)
    • a letter from sb. (来源)
    • be made of gold/from wheat (来源)
    • die of cancer (原因)
    • leave with a laugh (方式)
    • donate blood out of sympathy (动机)
