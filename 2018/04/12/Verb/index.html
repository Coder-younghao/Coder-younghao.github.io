Tense and aspect

Tense

  • Simple Present（do）

    • 表现在：Then you will come to a level plain, in which the Nile flows round an island names Tachompso.
    • 表过去：Martin Luther King delivers his last speech
    • 表将来：We’ll have to stay at home if it rains tomorrow

  • Simple Past（done）

    • 表现在：If you didn’t love him, you wouldn’t be so sad about the news.
    • 表过去：I wondered if you could help me
    • 表将来：Suppose they invited us this weekend.

Aspect

  • Present Progressive（be doing）：
    • 表现在：Take an umbrella. It’s raining outside
    • 表将来：I’m leaving tomorrow.
  • Past Progressive（was/were doing）
    • 表过去：We were still living in New York in 1959
    • 表现在：It’s time we were leaving
    • 表将来：I was hoping you could do us a favor
  • Present Perfect（have done）
    • John has lived here for ten years
  • Present Perfect Progressive（have been doing）
    • This couple have been living here since their marriage.
  • Past Perfect（had done）
    • He had lived here before I moved in
  • Past Perfect Progressive（had been doing）
    • He had been living here for ten years by the time he moved away
  • Future
    • be going to do，be about to do， be to do

总结：一般现在，一般过去，现在进行，过去进行在不同的语境中可以表示过去，现在，将来

Voice and Mood

Passive Voice

  • be-被动

    • The children were punished.

  • get-被动

    • The children got punished.

  • by-短语

    • He bought a book. = The book was bought by him.

  • 非谓语动词-被动

    • 不定式被动：He needs to be taken care of.
    • 现在分词被动：The building being repaired is our library.
    • 动名词被动：She likes being looked at.

Mood

  • be-型虚拟式

    • 主句含有表示意志的成分时，如一坚持 insist，二命令 order, command，三建议 advise, propose, suggest，四要求 demand, ask, request, require”以及 desire, prefer, urge, decide, move, recommend 等词时，从句谓语用 should+动词原形。

  • were-型虚拟式

    • 现在：If he were here, he would help me now
    • 过去：If I had known it earlier, I would have made preparations.
    • 将来：If he asked me (should/were to ask me), I would go with him.
    • 倒装：Were it not for their assistance, we couldn’t get over the difficulties

  • wish引导的宾从

    • I wish I were a free bird
    • I wish I had finished my task yesterday
    • I wish I would go to the cinema tomorrow.

  • 其他

    • He would rather die than give in.
    • It’s (high/almost) time that sb did sth.
    • He speaks English so well as if he were English.
    • She looked as if she had succeeded.

Modal auxiliaries

can&may

  • can
    • 能力：I can swim, but I can’t skate
    • 请求：Can I use your dictionary?
    • 在理论上存在的可能性：What can the children be doing?
  • may
    • 能力：You may go now
    • 更委婉的请求：May I smoke in this room？
    • 在现实中确实存在的可能性：The snake may be dangerous

will&shall

  • will

    • 请求，建议：Will / Would you pass me the ball, please?
    • 意志，愿望和决心：I will never do that again.
    • would表示过去反复发生的动作或某种倾向：During the vacation, he would visit me every other
      day.
    • 估计和猜想：It would be about ten o’ clock when she left home.

  • shall

    • 用于第一人称，征求对方意见：What shall we do this evening？
    • 用于第二三人称，表示给对方的命令，警告：You shall fail if you don‘t work hard

Non-finite Verbs

Infinitive

  • 不带to的不定式
    • had better/best do sth.(最好做某事)，cannot but do sth.(只能，不得不做某事)，couldn’t help but do sth. (忍不住做某事)，would rather do sth(. 宁愿做某事)，would rather do A than do B(宁愿做A也不愿做B)，may/might(just) as well do sth.(不妨，倒不如做某事)，would do nothing but do(只会做)。
  • 带to的不定式
    • 接不定式作宾语:agree, aim, apply, attempt, bother,(negative), care (negative), choose, claim, consent, decide, demand, desire, determine, expect, fail, guarantee, hesitate, hope, learn, manage, neglect, offer, plan, prepare, pretend, proceed, promise, prove, refuse, resolve, seek, swear, threaten, venture, volunteer, vow 等。
    • 接不定式作宾补:advise, allow, ask, believe, bribe, command, compel, consider, declare, discover, encourage, find, forbid, force, get, order, permit, persuade, prove, recommend, rely on, wait for, call on, remind, require, tell, urge 等。
      I believe him to be honest.
    • 接不定式作宾语或宾补:ask, cannot bear, hate, help, intend, like, prefer, want, wish 等。
      I prefer my roommate not to make any noise at night.
    • 固定句型:the+序数词(first/last)+ to do

Present Participle

  • 接现在分词作宾语:admit, acknowledge(承认), advocate, appreciate, avoid, consider, delay, deny, dislike, ensure, enjoy, escape, fancy, favor, finish, give up, imagine, include, involve, keep (on) , miss, pardon, postpone, practice, prevent,propose, put off, report, risk, stop, suggest, understand, cannot help doing

Past Participle

  • 接过去分词做主语补语:appear, be, become, feel ,get, go, grow, keep, look, remain, seem
    The work remains undone.
  • 接过去分词作宾补:have, get, keep, leave, make，find 等。如:have one’s car repaired, get the work finished, leave a lot of problems unsolved, make oneself heard, keep one’s mouth shut, find sth stolen.
